Family River Discovery

Duration: 3 hours | Difficulty: Easy

Perfect for families, this gentle river journey mixes calm stretches with playful rapids, ideal for children starting at seven years old. Our friendly guides focus on safety, fun, and confidence-building while parents relax and enjoy the scenery, wildlife, and laughter that fill every peaceful mile together. It is an easy introduction to rafting that builds smiles, skills, trust, and lasting memories. No experience required, just a sense of curiosity and excitement to share with everyon

Place: American River, California

Growing Rapids Challenge

Duration: 4 hours | Difficulty: Medium

This balanced adventure blends steady rapids with technical turns and splashing excitement for kids from twelve years and up. It challenges balance, timing, and teamwork without overwhelming first-timers, making it the perfect bridge between calm family floats and advanced whitewater runs. Expect energetic paddling, refreshing waves, and rewarding moments of achievement as you move through scenic canyons and lively current. Confidence grows quickly here, and every successful rapid builds skill, trust, and group momentum together.

Place: South Yuba River, California

Extreme Whitewater Assault

Duration: 5 hours | Difficulty: Hard

Created exclusively for experienced rafters, this demanding route delivers powerful Class IV rapids, narrow channels, and relentless drops that test endurance and technical skill. Every section demands fast decisions, precise paddle control, and total teamwork under pressure. The reward is an intense, high-speed descent through rugged wilderness where focus never fades and adrenaline drives every rapid, turn, and surge forward. Only those with solid river experience should attempt this challenge at full commitment and preparation.

Place: Tuolumne River, California
