Splah White Water Rafting

Splash White Water Rafting brings adventure seekers together for thrilling river experiences. Expert guides ensure safety, excitement, and unforgettable memories. Families, friends, and adventurers alike discover rapid thrills and serene stretches of nature. Our commitment to eco-friendly practices, quality equipment, and personalized service guarantees every trip is extraordinary. Join us to navigate roaring waters, embrace challenges, and celebrate teamwork in the wild. Every journey combines adrenaline, laughter, and breathtaking scenery, creating stories to cherish forever. Adventure, safety, and fun unite at Splash—where rivers meet excitement, passion, and unforgettable moments.

History

In the late 1970s, we—Mark, Tony, and Luis—explored rivers with fearless curiosity, seeking adventure and thrills together. From the very first rapid, we knew we wanted to share this excitement with others. Our early trips were simple, with wooden rafts and homemade gear, but every journey taught us about teamwork, trust, and respect for the wild waters.

As word spread, more friends and families joined our adventures. We formalized our company, Splash White Water Rafting, and committed to combining passion with professionalism. Today, decades later, our mission remains the same: to guide adventurers of all ages through thrilling rivers, honoring our legacy while creating new stories that celebrate nature, challenge, and the joy of shared adventure.
